Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Standard check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability. Guests in our Presidential Villa enjoy flexible check-in/check-out times as a complimentary service.
Breakfast is included with all Ocean View Suite and Presidential Villa bookings. For Deluxe Garden Rooms, breakfast can be added at Rp 350,000 per person per day. We offer a spectacular buffet breakfast at our Ocean Terrace restaurant from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM.
Yes, we offer premium airport transfer services in our luxury fleet. A one-way transfer from Ngurah Rai International Airport starts at $45 USD. For Presidential Villa guests, a complimentary round-trip transfer is included. Please provide your flight details at least 24 hours in advance.
We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in will incur a charge equivalent to one night's stay. Peak season bookings (July–August, December–January) may have different terms — please check at time of booking.
We are a pet-friendly resort for small dogs and cats (up to 10 kg) in our Presidential Villa. A refundable pet deposit of $200 is required. Unfortunately, pets are not permitted in other room categories or public areas. Please contact our concierge for the complete pet policy.
